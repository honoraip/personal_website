Green Tea Lava Cake

This dessert consists of a green tea flavored molten lava cake accompanied by a scoop of tofu flavored ice cream. Both items are delicious. When you cut open the lava cake, green tea flavored filling would ooze out and you are supposed to quickly scoope the outer cake with the filling and eat them together. The tofu ice cream is also very interesting. It tastes like frozen soy milk.
Cream Mochi

This dessert consists of sweet glutenous rice outer wrap with cream filling inside. I enjoyed the dish even though it felt a bit unhealthy as I was eating it.
Chicken Stew

This is a dish from a Portugese restaurant. The chicken tasted tender and the creamy white sauce complimented the meat very well. The leftover sauce can also be added to white rice and the sauce tastes very rich and delicious.
